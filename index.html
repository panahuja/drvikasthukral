    MBBS , MS - Ophthalmology , /
    Eye Surgeon , 15 Years Experience

    Dr. Vikas Thukral is a famous ophthalmologist practising in NCR. He has done his graduation (MBBS) and postraduation ( MS) from PGIMS, Rohtak. He also has to his credit a fellowship from the prestigious Sankara Nethralaya, Chennai. He is the Director of Faridabad Eye Care, Faridabad. Besides, he is a Senior Consultant ( Oculoplasty & Facial Aesthetics) with Max Healthcare. He Consults at Max hospitals in Panchsheel, N. Delhi, Gurgaon & Noida.
