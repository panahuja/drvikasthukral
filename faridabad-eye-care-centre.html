Faridabad Eye Care Centre

Faridabad eye care centre is an comprehensive eye care centre located in a calm and upscale neighbourhood in Faridabad.

Facilities available include:

  • Computerised eye check up
  • Slit lamp
  • Applanation tonometry
  • Retina check up
  • Indirect Ophthalmoscopy
  • Cataract surgery by phacoemuslification
  • Glaucoma screening and surgery
  • Squint checkup and surgery
  • Yag laser
  • Oculoplasty
  • Plastic Surgery of Eyelids

Facial Arsthetics:

  • Botox and Dermal fillers,
  • RF laser for mole and wart removal
  • Eye Bags Removal
  • Fully equipped operation theatre
  • Mediclaim facility and various panels available
